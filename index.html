Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my Grok chat as a PDF?

Once the extension is installed, open the Grok conversation you wish to save. You'll find an 'Export Chat' button added by the extension (usually near the chat input or options menu). Click it, select 'PDF' from the available formats, adjust any settings like page orientation or margins if needed, and then click 'Download'.

Can I export only part of a conversation?

Yes. While exporting the entire chat is the default, the extension will include options to select specific messages or a range of messages before you generate the export file. Look for checkboxes or selection controls within the export pop-up.

Is my Grok data safe?

Absolutely. Your privacy is paramount. Grok to PDF performs all processing directly within your browser. Your conversation data is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external servers. It stays entirely on your local machine.

What formats are supported?

You can export your Grok chats into several useful formats: PDF, Markdown (.md), HTML, plain text (.txt), JSON, and CSV. This allows flexibility for different use cases like documentation, sharing, or data analysis.

Does it work in Grok's light mode?

Yes. The extension is designed to detect whether you're using Grok's light or dark theme. When exporting formats like PDF or HTML, it will attempt to replicate the appearance, including syntax highlighting appropriate for the theme you were using.
